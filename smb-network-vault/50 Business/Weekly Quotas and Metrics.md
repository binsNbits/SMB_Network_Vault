# Weekly Quotas and Metrics

**Review every Friday, no exceptions.** These numbers determine everything — if MRR is behind plan ([[Financial Model]]), the fix is always at the top of the activity table, not in tooling or delivery.

## Activity quotas (leading indicators)

| Metric | Weekly target |
|---|---|
| New prospects added to list | 25 |
| Outreach touches (calls/walk-ins/emails) | 40 |
| Real conversations (2+ min with a decision-influencer) | 10 |
| [[Security Snapshot]] pitches delivered | 4 |
| Snapshots sold | 1 |

Rough conversion math: 40 touches → 10 conversations → 1–2 snapshots sold → 0.5–1 retainer/month early on, improving as referral partners kick in ([[Outreach Playbook]]).

## Dashboard (lagging indicators)

| Metric | Target | Red flag |
|---|---|---|
| MRR | Per ramp table in [[Financial Model]] | Two flat months in a row |
| Outreach touches this week | 40 | < 20 — delivery is eating sales time, the classic death spiral ([[Risks and Failure Modes]]) |
| Snapshot → retainer conversion | > 40% | < 25% — findings meeting or [[Retainer Tiers|pricing]] is broken |
| Avg revenue per client | ≥ $1,000/mo | Creeping down — signing too-small clients ([[Ideal Client Profile]]) |
| % revenue from top client | < 25% | One client can kill the business |
| Hours per client per month | < 6 | Over 10 hrs → price increase or a root-cause [[Project Pricing|project]] ([[Solo Operations and Boundaries]]) |
| Referral partners active | 5 by month 12 | 0 after month 6 |
