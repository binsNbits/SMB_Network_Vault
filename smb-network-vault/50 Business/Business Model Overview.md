# Business Model Overview

**The business:** Fractional IT / "MSP-lite" for small businesses (5–30 employees), with a cybersecurity-hardening angle and M365/Google Workspace administration as the core delivery skills. Solo founder, advanced IT experience, no startup capital, willing to do outbound sales.

**One-line pitch:** *"I'm the IT department for businesses too small to hire one — flat monthly rate, local, and I answer the phone."*

## The model in one paragraph

Sell a **monthly flat-rate retainer** ([[Retainer Tiers]]) covering helpdesk, patching, backups, security basics, and vendor wrangling. Land clients through a cheap, concrete **wedge offer** — the [[Security Snapshot]] or an email-deliverability fix ([[Email Deliverability Playbook]]) — deliver it fast and visibly well, then convert to the retainer. Ten retainers averaging $1,200/month is $144K/year gross with 10–15% costs ([[Financial Model]]). The business is won or lost on **sales conversations started per week** ([[Weekly Quotas and Metrics]]), not technical skill.
