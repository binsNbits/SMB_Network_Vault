# Ideal Client Profile

- **Size:** 5–30 employees, 5–40 computers. Below 5 they can't pay; above 30, real MSPs compete hard ([[Fractional IT vs Full MSP]]).
- **Geography:** within ~45 minutes' drive. **Local presence is the #1 differentiator** against national MSPs and offshore helpdesks.

## Traits that predict a "yes"

- No internal IT person — the "tech guy" is an owner's relative or the most computer-literate employee.
- On Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace but **nobody administers it** ([[M365 vs Google Workspace Strategy]]).
- Just failed/struggled with a [[Cyber Insurance Questionnaire]], or their premium jumped.
- Recently burned: ransomware scare, email compromise ([[Email Authentication]]), dead server, departed employee who "had all the passwords."
- Growing: new office ([[New Site Deployment]]), new hires, merging with another practice.
