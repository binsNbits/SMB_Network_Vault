# Tool Stack Upgrade Path

What replaces the [[Launch Tool Stack]], **in purchase order, each funded by revenue — not savings**.

1. *(Not software)* **E&O + general liability insurance** (~$600–1,200/yr) — at first paying client, not before, not later → [[Legal Insurance and Licensing]].
2. **Free AV → resold EDR** (Huntress, or Defender for Business bundled in M365 Business Premium) — ~$3–7/endpoint, billed inside the per-user price. First real security upgrade; also what [[Cyber Insurance Questionnaire|insurance questionnaires]] demand. Buy with the first retainer.
3. **Nothing → SaaS backup for M365/Workspace** (Afi, Dropsuite) — ~$3–4/user. Required to deliver the Secure tier honestly — quarterly test restores are in the tier promise ([[Retainer Tiers]], [[Backup Strategy]]). Buy with the first Secure-tier client.
4. **Action1 + Zammad/shared-mailbox + spreadsheet → real PSA/RMM combo** (Syncro, ~$140/mo) — around **client #6–8**, when the free stack strains. One purchase consolidates four functions: RMM/patching, ticketing, invoicing/recurring billing, and remote access.
5. **Manual training → phishing simulation platform** (~$1–2/user) — when Secure-tier clients reach 2–3.

## Worth upgrading later (not in the original plan)

- **Obsidian/Notion → MSP documentation platform** (Hudu ~$31/user/mo, or IT Glue) around 8–10 clients — links credentials, assets, and runbooks per client; supports the "a stranger could cover for you" resilience goal ([[Risks and Failure Modes]], [[Documentation Standards]]).
- **HubSpot Free → keep it.** CRM is rarely the bottleneck; don't pay for pipeline software before 15 clients.

## Keep and never replace

Bitwarden (also a resale line), the e-signature free tier, and your own M365 Business Basic tenant — upgrade your own seat to Business Premium when you start reselling it, so you demo what you sell.

> [!warning] The financial guardrail
> Tool COGS must stay under ~$15/user against $95–135/user revenue — **85%+ gross margin**. Anything that doesn't fit (enterprise SIEM, $300+/mo PSAs) waits.
